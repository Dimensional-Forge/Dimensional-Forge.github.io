About

The Forge Is the Philosophy

Sean Neal is a Senior Systems Engineer and product development consultant based in Indianapolis. Background spans NASA Marshall Space Flight Center, Tesla (two internships), SonaCare Medical, and 8 years delivering connected hardware products at Allegion.

The Dimensional Forge is a consulting practice for inventors, startups, and small businesses that need senior engineering leadership — end-to-end, from initial concept through architecture, prototyping, validation, and manufacturing readiness.

One engineer. Full lifecycle. No hand-holding required from your side.
